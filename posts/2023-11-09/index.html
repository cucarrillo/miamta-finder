First Sighting (NC)

9 Nov 2023

Today was the lucky day where I finally found my first Miata!

I found myself an Silver 2008 NC while driving with my brother.

This being my first encounter I wanted to just take the pictures and leave, however my brother suggested that we should stay and talk to the owner. Im glad I did wait as the conversation we had was wonderful and hearing her talk about her Miata made me proud to be an owner of one as well. She had talked to us about how she had been run off the road in her Miata but despite that the NC was shining!

This is only the second time I have seen an NC up close and seeing the differances and similarities was cool.

Here are the pictures I took on my beloved Sony Mavica, and of course the polaroid I took of them. The worst picture is sadly the polaroid, thats just something that Im gonna have to get better at.
