A Small Update (NA)

6 Feb 2024

Those who know me personally, know that I got rear-ended in my Miata (damage pictured below). This happened a few days before Thanksgiving of 2023. So because of that, the driving of my NA was brought down to once a month (just so I know it still works).

I should be taking my NA to get repaired in the coming weeks after getting a big delay. I wanted to post an updated without finding another Miata. I feel like this would go against the spirit of the website. Luckily I found one today, a white 1989 NA.

Once again my brother was with me and we did our thing. We had planned to leave when the polaroid we took developed (and leave it on the car) but we were happy to see that the owner came out and we had a small chat. He told us that he had two Miatas and he only drove the white NA today because it had been sitting for months. He showed us his pictures of his other Miata (also a NA) and we gave him the polaroid we took.
